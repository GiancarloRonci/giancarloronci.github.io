# Giancarlo Ronci

## Data & Analytics Coach & Advisory

Italy · Florence / Rome area

For coaching, advisory, speaking or exchanges on Data, Analytics and AI, feel free to reach out on LinkedIn.

## Bio

Giancarlo Ronci is a senior data leader and advisor with extensive experience in data management, data warehousing, analytics engineering and modern cloud data platforms.

He works at the intersection of business, architecture and delivery, helping organizations turn data into concrete operating capabilities. His background combines hands-on experience in data warehouse design, streaming and batch pipelines, data governance, data quality and analytics enablement.

His approach is pragmatic: connect strategy, operating model, architecture and execution to generate measurable business value.

## Main areas of activity

- Building and evolving modern data platforms
- Defining pragmatic Data Strategy and operating models
- Improving Data Governance and Data Quality capabilities
- Enabling Business Intelligence, Advanced Analytics and AI adoption
- Coaching professionals and teams on Data & Analytics leadership
